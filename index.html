Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffe is a South Korean fantasy recipe!!!! This dish went viral on WhatsApp all across the world during Covid-19. It was treated as a Lockdown activity lifting people's boredom. It is very delicous and easy to make.

About Chef

Chef Mahika is a very good chef. Even though she has never tried this recipe she is eager to help us.

Ingredients

  • Hot Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Coffee
Step 1: Add hot water and coffee powder and whisk
Add a cup of hot water, 2 tablespoons of coffee powder and 3 tablespoons of sugar

Step 2: Whisk Whisk Whisk and Whisk some more until you get a nice fluffy texture
Use a hand mixer to whisk the coffe powder,sugar,and hot water mixture, till it turns to a fluffy creamy mixture

Step 3: Serve with hot milk and a straw
Take a cup of hot/cold milk and and the smooth creamy coffe cream on top. Serve with spoon and straw. Enjoy your homemade Lockdown Recipe.
